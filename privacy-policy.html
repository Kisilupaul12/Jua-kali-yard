Privacy & Security Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

At Jua Kali Yard, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy outlines our data collection practices, security measures, and your rights under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

1. Data Collection and Privacy Statement

What Personal Data We Collect:

  • Name and Contact Information: Collected solely for order processing, delivery coordination, and customer service communication.
  • Phone Number: Used exclusively for SMS order updates and last-mile delivery coordination. Required for M-Pesa payment notifications.
  • Email Address: Collected for order confirmations, shipping notifications, and optional marketing communications (you can opt-out anytime).
  • Delivery Address: Necessary for product delivery only. We do not share your address with third parties except our trusted courier partners.

Our Privacy Commitment:

We will NEVER sell your personal data. Your information is used only for the purposes stated above. We do not share customer data with third-party marketers or advertisers without your explicit opt-in consent, in full compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

2. Transaction Security Commitment

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your payment information and ensure safe transactions.

Confidentiality

All payment information is secured using 256-bit SSL encryption (HTTPS) throughout the entire website, especially during checkout and login. Sensitive data like credit card numbers and M-Pesa PINs are never stored on our servers.

Integrity

We ensure that order details (quantity, price, product specifications) cannot be altered during transmission or storage by unauthorized parties. All transactions are logged securely for audit purposes.

Non-repudiation

The M-Pesa transaction confirmation SMS and email receipts provide digital proof of payment with unique Transaction IDs, serving as undeniable evidence for both buyer and seller. This prevents disputes about order placement or payment.

Authenticity

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) using One-Time PINs (OTP) sent to your registered phone number before finalizing high-value purchases (above Ksh 20,000) ensures that you are who you claim to be.

3. System Reliability and Availability

99.9% Uptime Commitment

We maintain redundant cloud servers with automatic failover. If our main server experiences issues, backup servers immediately take over to prevent downtime, ensuring the website and payment systems are always accessible.

Digital Certificates and Authenticity

Our website uses verified SSL/TLS certificates (shown by the padlock icon üîí in your browser) to guarantee that you are on the real Jua Kali Yard site, protecting you from phishing attacks and spoofed websites.

Legal Compliance

Jua Kali Yard fully complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction as mandated by Section 38 of the DPA.

Your Rights

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
